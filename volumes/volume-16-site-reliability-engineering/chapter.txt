📘 Volume 16 — Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)
🎯 Objective

Learn the principles and practices of Site Reliability Engineering to design, operate, maintain, and continuously improve highly available, scalable, and reliable production systems.

Outcome:
After completing this volume, you should be able to manage production environments using reliability engineering principles, respond to incidents, plan capacity, improve system resilience, and balance innovation with stability.
