📘 Volume 02 — Computer Architecture
🎯 Objective

Build a solid understanding of how a computer works from the moment you press the power button until an application is running.

Outcome:
After completing this volume, you should understand the internal components of a computer, how the operating system interacts with hardware, how programs execute, how memory works, and why Linux and servers exist.
