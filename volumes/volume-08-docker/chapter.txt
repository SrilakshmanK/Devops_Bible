📘 Volume 08 — Containers & Docker
🎯 Objective

Understand containerization from first principles and master Docker for building, running, networking, securing, and managing containers in production.

Outcome:
After completing this volume, you should understand how containers work internally, build optimized Docker images, manage multi-container applications, troubleshoot container issues, and prepare for Kubernetes.
