📘 Volume 15 — DevSecOps
🎯 Objective

Integrate security into every stage of the DevOps lifecycle by learning how to secure infrastructure, applications, containers, CI/CD pipelines, Kubernetes, and cloud environments.

Outcome:
After completing this volume, you should be able to design, implement, automate, and maintain secure DevOps workflows while following modern DevSecOps practices.
