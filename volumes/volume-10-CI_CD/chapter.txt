📘 Volume 10 — Continuous Integration & Continuous Delivery (CI/CD)
🎯 Objective

Master the principles, tools, and workflows of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery/Deployment by designing, building, securing, and maintaining production-grade CI/CD pipelines.

Outcome:
After completing this volume, you should be able to build automated pipelines that compile, test, package, scan, and deploy applications using Jenkins and GitHub Actions while following modern DevOps best practices.
